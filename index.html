🌱 Instructions for Your New Plant

Step 1

Pull the tab from underneath the pot that says "pull".

Step 2

When you pull the tab it will open up the bottom. Then you can plant it directly into the soil, including the box, because it will decompose and turn into mulch.

Step 3 💧 Watering

Water your plant regularly to keep the soil moist but not too wet. Make sure the water reaches the roots and avoid overwatering.

🌿 Take care of your plant and watch it grow!
